About This Package

Tokyo is unlike anywhere else on earth — a city where a 800-year-old shrine stands in the shadow of a neon-lit skyscraper, where a bowl of ramen can be a religious experience, and where bullet trains glide silently between worlds. Ten nights gives you just enough time to scratch the surface.

Your 4-star hotel in Shinjuku puts you at the centre of everything. The included 7-day JR rail pass means you can escape the city too — Kyoto, Hiroshima and Mount Fuji are all within easy reach and all covered by the pass.
