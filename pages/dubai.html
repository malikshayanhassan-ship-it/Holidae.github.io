About This Package

Dubai is one of the most extraordinary cities on earth — a place where a camel market sits a few miles from the world's tallest building. This 5-night city break packs in the best of both: the glittering skyline and the golden desert.

You'll be staying at a 5-star hotel in the heart of the city, steps from the Dubai Mall and within sight of the Burj Khalifa. Your included desert safari takes you out into the dunes for a sunset camel ride and a traditional Bedouin dinner under the stars.
