Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you book

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team — we're happy to help.

🗓️ Booking

Booking is simple. Browse our packages page, click "View Deal" on the package you love, then fill in your departure date and number of travellers in the booking box. Once you click "Book Now" a member of our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm and take payment.

We recommend booking at least 8–12 weeks in advance to get the best prices and flight availability. That said, we sometimes have last-minute deals available — check our packages page or get in touch.

💳 Payments

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as bank transfer. All online transactions are secured by 256-bit SSL encryption.

Not at all. We ask for a 20% deposit at the time of booking to secure your package and flights. The remaining balance is due 10 weeks before your departure date. We'll send you a reminder well in advance.

Yes — every Holidae booking is fully ATOL protected. This means if anything happens to us as a company, the Civil Aviation Authority will ensure you receive a full refund or that your holiday is completed as planned. You'll receive an ATOL certificate at the time of booking.

❌ Cancellations & Changes

Cancellations made more than 10 weeks before departure receive a full refund of the deposit. Between 10–4 weeks you'll receive 50% of the total package cost back. Cancellations within 4 weeks of departure are non-refundable, though we strongly recommend travel insurance to cover this.

Date changes are subject to flight and hotel availability. A £50 admin fee applies per change. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to amend your booking — earlier is always easier.

✈️ Packages & What's Included

All of our current packages include return flights from London Heathrow unless stated otherwise on the package page. If you are travelling from a different departure airport, get in touch and we'll do our best to arrange alternatives.
