About This Package

Few places on earth match the drama of Santorini — volcanic cliffs tumbling into the deep blue Aegean, villages of white cubic houses and cobalt-blue domes clinging to the caldera rim. This 5-night package puts you right in the heart of it all.

You'll be staying at a hand-picked 5-star boutique hotel in Oia, widely considered the most beautiful village on the island. Your room comes with a private terrace and a view of the famous Santorini sunset — one of the most celebrated in the world.
